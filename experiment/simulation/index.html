Instructions

  1. Click on CREATE to perform the experiment.
  2. Enter the values for X(Age) and Y(Glucose-level) in the Training Data Set and click on the ADD button to add values to the table.
  3. Repeat step 2 until there are 10 rows in the table.
  4. Click on the NEXT button to proceed further.
  5. Enter a value for X(Age) in Testing Data Set and then click on the NEXT button.
  6. Complete the observation table by clicking on the buttons labeled XY, X² and Σ in the specified order.
  7. Enter values for b0 equation, then click on the SUBMIT button.
  8. Enter values for b1 equation, then click on the SUBMIT button.
  9. Enter the values of b0 and b1 into the y equation, and then click on the SUBMIT button.
  10. Finally, click the PLOT button to plot the graph.
